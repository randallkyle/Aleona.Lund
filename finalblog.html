TIPS AND ADVICE FOR STARTERS
  1. Start with crayolas and try to mix colors
  2. use more than just your colored pencils or brushes, find other things you could use outside.
  3. remember that great art takes hard work.
  4. lastly, remember that nothing is set in stone
